Interactions and Errata

In a game with 238 unique alien powers and 7 expansions, rule conflicts and written errors were bound to pop up. Below is an official errata document, containing relevant information until 2018. The document doesn't have anything pertaining to 2022's Cosmic Oddysey -expansion, but it will still serve the majority of players just fine.

But in case you wanted an even more detailed encyclopedia of Cosmic Encounter with new infromation included, I highly recommend checking out the excellently written Cosmodex 2.0 by Bill Martinson.
